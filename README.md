# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI-Employee-Attrition-Workforce-Insights
A visually interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, demographics, job roles, salary ranges, tenure, and education trends. The dashboard helps HR teams identify attrition patterns, high-risk segments, and data-driven workforce decisions.
# HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

This repository contains a complete **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, designed to help organizations analyze employee attrition and identify workforce trends.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides insights into:

- Total Employees  
- Total Attrition  
- Attrition Rate  
- Average Age  
- Average Salary  
- Average Tenure  
- Attrition by Age Group  
- Attrition by Education  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition by Monthly Income  
- Attrition by Tenure  
- Gender-based attrition  
- Department-level filters  

It helps HR teams answer business questions like:

- Which age group has the highest attrition?
- Which job roles are most affected?
- Is salary influencing attrition?
- How does education level impact retention?
- What is the tenure-wise attrition pattern?

## 📈 Key Insights

- The **26–35 age group** has the highest attrition.  
- Employees with **Life Sciences & Medical** backgrounds show higher exit rates.  
- **Laboratory Technicians** and **Sales Executives** have the highest attrition among job roles.  
- Attrition is highest among employees earning **up to 5K monthly income**.  
- The first **1–2 years** show the highest exit rate, signaling early job dissatisfaction.
